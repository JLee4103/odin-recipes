Pita!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders
  • Za'atar Seasoning
  • Lemons x2
  • olive oil
  • chickpeas
  • yogurt
  • garlic
  • seasoning
  • pita bread
  • cucumbers
  • onions

Steps

  1. Season the raw chicken tenders with Za'atar seasoning, half a lemon, and olive oil
  2. Cook chick peas with oil and Za'atar seasoning
  3. Cook seasoned chicken after cooking chick peas to a crisp
  4. Create a sauce with a mix of yogurt, garlic, and red seasoning pepper
  5. toast pita bread

Plating

  1. Lay out pita bread and coat with yogurt sauce
  2. add chicken, chickpeas, cucumbers, and onions
